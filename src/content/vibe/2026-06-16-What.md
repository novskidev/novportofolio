The most impactful and worth moving from next.js to astro is how the light and fast astro is.

when iam build my portofolio with next.js, i wasnt calculate or burden with bundle size or hydration - as long everything work well. Everything look normal until i meet lot of trouble when maintaining daily my next.js portofolio

in the few month seems normal until i found few burden thing such as slow performance, peak at lighthouse score, everytime i build the bundle took ekstra time longer. I need time to optimize my mdx file, since next.js not native for creating mdx files. So i decide to Astro.

Astro bring new insight for maintaining static website. its faster performance , mdx friendly, island architecture, framework agnostic, and lighter build output. I think this is best for static mdx portofolio site

doesnt mean next.js bad, its good in the certain of areas. i still use next.js for maintaining school site that need SSR and connection to CMS. but for portofolio for showingcase my project and blogging. Astro is better !

The key is pick the right tool for the right job.
